How We Work

From Idea to App Store

A clear, collaborative process — you are involved at every step, so there are no surprises.

01
You Share Your Idea & Features
Tell us what you want to build — your idea, the features you need, and who it is for. No tech knowledge required.
02
Design & Prototype
We create wireframes and an interactive prototype so you can see and feel the app before a single line of production code is written.
03
Full App Development
We build your complete iOS or macOS app using Swift and SwiftUI, following Apple's Human Interface Guidelines for a native feel.
04
Testing & Refinement
Thorough testing across devices and OS versions. We fix bugs, polish interactions, and make sure everything feels great.
05
App Store Publishing (Optional)
We handle the full submission process — screenshots, descriptions, metadata, and Apple review compliance — so your app launches smoothly.
